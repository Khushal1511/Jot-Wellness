School inclusion works best when the ecosystem is prepared before the first day becomes overwhelming.

Readiness is not only about sitting at a desk. It includes transitions, toileting, snack routines, sensory comfort, peer play, attention, safety, and teacher communication.

## A practical readiness checklist

- What routines will be hardest for the child?
- What visual supports can reduce uncertainty?
- Which teacher needs a short strategy note?
- What should parents practise at home before school starts?
- How will success be measured in the first month?

A prepared ecosystem gives the child more than admission. It gives them belonging.
