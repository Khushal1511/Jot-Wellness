Home practice should not make parents feel like they must become therapists all day.

The most sustainable progress often comes from small repeatable moments already present in family life.

## Start with one routine

Choose one daily routine such as brushing, snack time, getting dressed, bedtime, or leaving for school. Add one simple goal to that routine.

For example:

- Offer two choices during snack time
- Practise one transition warning before leaving the house
- Use a visual first-then card before bedtime
- Pause during play and wait for the child to request more

## Keep it human

A home is still a home. Progress should protect warmth, play, rest, and connection.
